Renewable energy development.

FAWT Energy Limited is an Isle of Man incorporated company engaged in the development of well-established renewable energy technologies, alongside emerging solutions supporting long-term decarbonisation.

Our core business includes onshore wind, solar and anaerobic digestion, as well as biochar—pioneering the next generation of green power solutions.
