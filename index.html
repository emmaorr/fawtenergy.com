Delivering renewable energy projects from concept to energisation.

FAWT Energy Limited is a renewable energy developer active across Ireland and Northern Ireland. We originate and deliver projects across onshore wind, solar and anaerobic digestion, alongside biochar initiatives supporting the decarbonisation of heat and power.

Our work spans the full development lifecycle: identifying sites, engaging with landowners and stakeholders, managing the planning and consenting process, undertaking environmental assessments, securing grid access, and overseeing delivery through construction and commissioning.
