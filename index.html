Overview

Delivering renewable energy projects from concept to energisation.

FAWT Energy Limited is a renewable energy developer active across Ireland and Northern Ireland. We originate and deliver projects across onshore wind, solar and anaerobic digestion, alongside biochar initiatives supporting the decarbonisation of heat and power.

Our work spans the full development lifecycle: identifying sites, engaging with landowners and stakeholders, managing the planning and consenting process, undertaking environmental assessments, securing grid access, and overseeing delivery through construction and commissioning.

What we do

End-to-end development

We manage renewable energy development through each stage, including:

  • Site identification and feasibility
  • Landowner engagement and option and lease progression
  • Consenting strategy and planning applications, including environmental assessments where required
  • Grid connection strategy and application management
  • Procurement support, construction oversight and commissioning through to energisation

Repowering and optimisation

Repowering experience

FAWT has repowered multiple wind turbine assets, extending operational life while improving performance and efficiency. Repowering projects are progressed through appropriate planning and grid processes, ensuring compliance and long-term asset optimisation.

Technology focus

  • Onshore wind (including repowering)
  • Solar PV
  • Anaerobic digestion
  • Biochar

Active development pipeline

A number of large-scale projects are scheduled for progression during 2026, reflecting a substantial and active development pipeline across Ireland and Northern Ireland.
